Vikram's Story

A Digital Oasis

By Vikram Singh

This is a space designed to make the world a better place. And for massive amounts of fun. And hard work. My name is Vikram Singh and I am a student at the MITxPro Fullstack MERN bootcamp. This full-time course is designed to give students a market-ready approach to both learning *how to learn code* as well as full stack.

I come from a journalism background with recent experience in the arts and entertainment industry. I currently work with the Starfish Accelerator Foundation, an organization dedicated to bridging the 'narrative culture gap' that exists within the USA.

I grew up in Silicon Valley at the height of the dotcom boom in the 90s. I witnessed the power of journalism first hand at a 24-hour broadcast news station in India. I fell back in love with the arts when I moved to Los Angeles. And now? I hope to round out my skillsets with a solid foundation in the fundamentals of web development.

I hope this space can grow to be a place where useful things are used, fun times are found, and perhaps a perspective or two gets challenged along the way.
