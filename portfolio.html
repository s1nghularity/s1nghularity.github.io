A Past Life

Mapping all the bus stops going from Sainik Farms to New Delhi Television in South New Delhi. As an in-house gopher for the newsdesk I rode this route every day (sometimes multiple times a day) from 2009 to 2012. Using a for loop to run through all the coordinates, Mapbox for everything else, with a button borrowed and modified from UIverse.
